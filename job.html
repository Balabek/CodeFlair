Application Process

1

Apply

If you wan to apply to one of our open positions, just write an email with your CV and some words about you to jobs.mk@codeflair.ch

2

Interview 1

After we reviewed your application and you are matching the profile we are looking for, we will invite you to a first interview at our office. You will get introduced to our team and see the office spaces as well talk with our local manager to see if you fit for the role and to the team.

3

Test Task

If the first interview went well, you will receive a small programming task within some days. The task should not require more then 2-3 hours of effort and gives us a good insight in your skills.

4

Interview 2

As soon as we reviewed your task results you get an invitation to a 2nd interview where we go more into the technical details. You will be asked to answer questions about your programming task results as well we will talk about the technical details of the position you applied.

5

Job Offer

If all went well, this is the time you will receive a formal offer (contract) from our side which you will hopefully agree and join our great team in Skopje.
